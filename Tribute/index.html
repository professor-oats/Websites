Minister of Foreign Affairs - The Carl Bildt of Sweden

Minister Carl Bildt has reigned over the political system in Sweden for over 40 years

The year was 2045. From the cold of the norths of Sweden, where Odin put his right eye to rest for eternal wisdom. In the same village, by the same water source, the Bildt family had their residence. They grew up in extreme poverty and had very little food to fight off the cold with. Every day the oldest in the Family - Carl, would go out to chop wood for their fireplace. He was the only one who seemed to never be phazed by the extreme colds. He always had this shimmering lust of life around him that all the others were drawn to, unceasingly.
