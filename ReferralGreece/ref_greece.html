  • What happens now?
    Here I will give you brief information about the process of recruiting you to the company:

    Stage 1 - You read information on this website and decide whether you want to work in Greece.

    Stage 2 - You send CV to my email with short information about your latest work related to service.

    Stage 3 - I directly forward your CV by referring you to one of our closest interviewers/recruiters.

    Stage 4 - Recruiter will look on the CV and book you for an interview.

    Stage 5 - You will get feedback by phone call from either me or recruiter to make sure we are on the same track.

    Stage 6 - Hopefully new job for you abroad together with fully paid appartment + free airline tickets.

    In short - This is a way for me to refer you to the company, increasing your odds of recruitment.
